Instructions

Our Home page consist of -

i. Vision and Mission and information about departments of college.

ii. About us section provides information about our website.

User Section:

If you are a new user create the account by clicking on sign up and then login else click on login. Once login successfully user will be redirected to user home page through which user can perform process to access bonafide.

Steps to access bonafide certificate

i. Select the reason for requirement of bonafide certificate.

ii. Fill the details and download the application form.

iii. Upload the required documents along with downloaded application form.

iv. User must logout after the completion of process.

User will receive the bonafide certificate once approved by admin.

Admin :

i. If request approved, bonafide certificate will be sent to user's registered email

ii. If request rejected, the reason of rejection will be sent to user's registered email

About DIVS Bonafide Generator

DIVS Bonafide Generator is specially designed for students. Our website is initiative to provide solution to the problems faced by students while accessing bonafide certificate. Bonafide certificate is one of the important document required for many important forms but accessing it is very lengthy and time consuming process. By considering all these problems Our website creates a platform as per students and management convenience

Why 'DIVS' ?

To access bonafide certificate students need to submit all the documents to college office manually and need to check the status everyday till he/she receives it. During covid 19 pandemic, education was conducted in online mode but students need to go to college office for getting bonafide certificate. Our website save student's time, provide students with certificate as early as possible, avoiding to visit office repeatively, and for proper management of data required while issuing certificate.
